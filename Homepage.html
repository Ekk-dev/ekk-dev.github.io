about

I attend Hudson Valley Community College. My journey in graphic design has been driven by curiosity and a interest to learn various mediums to create art . Over time, I have honed my skills in Photoshop and other design software, constantly seeking new techniques and inspiration to refine my craft. My work reflects a commitment to creativity, attention to detail, and a love for exploring the endless possibilities of visual design.
